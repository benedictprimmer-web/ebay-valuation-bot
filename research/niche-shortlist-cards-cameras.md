## Card niches

### PSA 10 Panini Prizm Premier League (2023/24, top players)
**Thesis:** graded PL soccer cards (Salah, Haaland) are hot — plentiful sales, rising demand ahead of the World Cup.

- **Liquidity:** High. PL cards sell frequently, especially star rookies (PSA 10 Salah Pink Mosaic sold ~$39).
- **Fixed condition:** Yes — PSA-graded Gem Mint 10.
- **Real data:** Good — HobbyScan, PSA, and many sold eBay comps.
- **Price distribution:** Tight for common parallels (tens of dollars); 1/5 or auto parallels vary widely. Bargains appear when listings expire or dealers misprice.
- **Margin:** Challenging — many deals posted near market; need 25% gap after ~12% fees and postage. Top stars have slimmer margins.
- **Auth risk:** Low — PSA slabbed; counterfeit PSA cards exist but uncommon.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Strongly positive — World Cup 2026; new-season releases.
- **Price band:** ~£20–100 common parallels; rare inserts/autos £100+.
- **Comp examples:** Salah Pink Mosaic PSA 10 ~$39; Messi Prizm WC PSA 10 ~$175.
- **Main risk:** Low margins from competition; consignment scams (use known sellers).
- **Search/watch:** "2023 Panini Prizm Premier League PSA 10 (player)"; "PSA 10 Prizm Premier League Salah".

### PSA 10 Pokémon Charizard (Shining Fates SV107 etc.)
**Thesis:** popular graded Charizards in recent sets sell consistently — many watchers, stable asking prices.

- **Liquidity:** High — among the most-watched Pokémon cards (1,300+ listings for "Charizard VMAX PSA 10").
- **Fixed condition:** Yes — PSA 10, uniform.
- **Real data:** Good — many sold listings; Charizard VMAX (Shining Fates SV107) ~£222.
- **Price distribution:** Moderate — common PSA 10 VMAX ~£130–300; ultra-rares spike (out of scope).
- **Margin:** Mixed — mid-range absolute profit decent, same fee %. Hunt underpriced auctions / Best Offer.
- **Auth risk:** Very low — PSA slabs; buy via normal channels.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Stable-to-rising — Pokémon stays strong; Charizard iconic.
- **Price band:** ~£120–300 for PSA 10 V/VMAX.
- **Comp examples:** Shining Fates SV107 PSA 10 ~£222; Darkness Ablaze VMAX PSA 10 ~£135.
- **Main risk:** Fee compression on pricier cards; hype swings; avoid private scams.
- **Search/watch:** "PSA 10 Charizard VMAX Shining Fates SV107"; "PSA 10 Charizard SV107/SV122".

### PSA 10 Cristiano Ronaldo 2014 World Cup (Panini Prizm, #161)
**Thesis:** graded rookie-era WC card of an all-time star, with demonstrated collector value.

- **Liquidity:** Moderate — less frequent than modern parallels, but strong Ronaldo interest.
- **Fixed condition:** Yes — PSA 10.
- **Real data:** Available — SportscardsPro shows PSA 10 ~$395 (~£310) for 2014 Prizm WC #161.
- **Price distribution:** Tight high range — ~$300–400 PSA 10. Few bargains; watch auctions.
- **Margin:** Difficult — buying a key card 25% under £300 needs luck (overlooked listings, lots).
- **Auth risk:** Very low — slabbed; avoid misrepresented/altered cards.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Strong — Ronaldo perennial icon; WC hype may lift legacy cards.
- **Price band:** ~£250–350 PSA 10 (~$400).
- **Comp examples:** 2014 Prizm WC Ronaldo PSA 10 ~$395.
- **Main risk:** High price means significant fees; saturation if many slabs appear.
- **Search/watch:** "PSA 10 Ronaldo 2014 Prizm World Cup #161".

## Camera / lens niches

### Sony α7 III (ILCE-7M3, body only, used)
**Thesis:** full-frame mirrorless workhorse; high demand and ample history make pricing predictable.

- **Liquidity:** High — many listings/sales; 54 live listings, dozens of Best Offers.
- **Fixed condition:** Model fixed; body-only items comparable (mostly used/mint).
- **Real data:** Abundant — used bodies ~£800–1,000 (£814–£993); UsedLens confirms band.
- **Price distribution:** Moderate — good condition ~£800, heavy-use ~£570. Few sub-£700 deals.
- **Margin:** Moderate — source ~£600–700 to resell near £800 after ~12% fee + shipping.
- **Auth risk:** Low — not counterfeit; check for stolen/lost; prefer high-feedback sellers.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Steady — full-frame mirrorless stays popular.
- **Price band:** ~£750–1,000 used bodies.
- **Comp examples:** Mint body ~£814; heavily-used (145k clicks) ~£570.
- **Main risk:** Slim margins if mispriced; bundles; 13% FVF + ~£20 shipping.
- **Search/watch:** "Sony A7 III body used"; "Sony ILCE-7M3 Grade A".

### Canon EOS 5D Mark IV (body-only, used)
**Thesis:** veteran pro DSLR with a large base; used bodies trade at predictable prices.

- **Liquidity:** Good — numerous used listings, many BIN/Best Offer (£599–£899).
- **Fixed condition:** Yes (body-only); grip vs none.
- **Real data:** Clear — cluster ~£600–900; e.g. £599.95 and £899.00, median ~£750.
- **Price distribution:** Moderate — "like new" top end, "good used" ~£600; auctions ~£500 occasionally.
- **Margin:** Moderate — pay ~£500–600 to profit from ~£750 sale; achievable via auctions/offers.
- **Auth risk:** Low — genuine Canon; serial checkable.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Stable — mature tech; still-photographer demand.
- **Price band:** ~£600–900 used.
- **Comp examples:** £599.95; £899.00.
- **Main risk:** 13% fee on ~£750 (~£100); verify shutter count.
- **Search/watch:** "Canon 5D Mark IV body used"; "EOS 5D Mark IV second hand".

### Canon EF 24–70mm f/2.8L II USM (used)
**Thesis:** top-tier Canon standard zoom (mk II) with enduring value; often sold near £690 with lots of comps.

- **Liquidity:** High — classic must-have; 97 used listings (filter out the f/4).
- **Fixed condition:** Yes (II model); used condition varies.
- **Real data:** Yes — £699.99 and £689.99 listings; offers ~£550–650.
- **Price distribution:** Narrow for mk II: ~£570–700. Mk I often ~£572.
- **Margin:** Thin — source ~£500 to resell ~£680 (25% gap); via lots/cosmetic issues.
- **Auth risk:** Low — genuine optics; check fungus/damage on heavy use.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Flat — steady pro/amateur demand.
- **Price band:** ~£570–700 (f/2.8L II).
- **Comp examples:** £699.99; £689.99.
- **Main risk:** Tight margins vs ~13% fee; rare returns hurt small profits.
- **Search/watch:** "Canon 24-70 2.8 Mark II used"; "EF 24–70mm f/2.8L II USM".

### Nikon F3 (35mm film SLR body, used)
**Thesis:** classic pro film camera with collectible appeal; bodies ~£250, higher with accessories.

- **Liquidity:** Moderate — film-enthusiast niche; several listings/sales.
- **Fixed condition:** Yes (F3 body); often "mint"; durable.
- **Real data:** Reasonable — bodies ~£250 (e.g. £250.21); kit deals higher.
- **Price distribution:** Narrow — most £220–300; auction bids ~£250.
- **Margin:** Small — buy ~£180, sell ~£250 (~30% target); deals from Asia/lots.
- **Auth risk:** Minimal — genuine Nikon; serial present.
- **Trend/catalyst:** Mildly positive — retro film trend; stable value.
- **Price band:** ~£220–300 good used.
- **Comp examples:** Body-only £250.21; bundles up to ~£400.
- **Main risk:** Tight margins; overseas shipping (£30–40); slower sell-through.
- **Search/watch:** "Nikon F3 body used"; "Nikon F3 FILM SLR tested".
